# Roles and Responsibilities

### Roles

#### Document Owner

- Draft, review, and update the document.

- Ensure the document is accurate, complete, and compliant with applicable regulations.

- Initiate document revisions when changes are required.

- Ensure all records associated with the document are generated and maintained according to this procedure.

#### Quality Manager

- Approve documents prior to release, ensuring compliance with applicable regulatory requirements.

- Maintain the Master Document List.

- Control document distribution, obsolescence, and archiving.

- Ensure document integrity, traceability, legibility, and retrievability.

- Perform internal audits to verify compliance with document and record control requirements.

#### Document Control

- Manage document issuance, access control, version control, and removal of obsolete documents.

- Ensure that only current and approved versions are available at points of use.

- Control editing rights and access privileges.

#### Regulatory Affairs

- Verify that documents affected by regulatory changes are updated accordingly.

- Inform QA of new or revised regulatory requirements impacting the QMS documentation.

#### Top Management

- Ensure that adequate resources are available for effective document and record control.

- Approve high-level QMS documents (e.g., Quality Policy, Quality Manual, and critical procedures).

#### Employees / End Users

- Access and use only current and approved documents.

- Follow documents as written.

- Report obsolete documents, discrepancies, or inconsistencies.
