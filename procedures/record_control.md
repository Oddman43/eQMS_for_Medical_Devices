# Roles and Responsibilities 

#### Document Owner

- Define which records must be generated as evidence of the process under their responsibility.

- Ensure that records are completed correctly (legible, indelible, accurate, and complete).

- Review generated records and verify their integrity before submitting them to QA or Document Control for archiving.

- Define applicable retention periods for records, based on regulatory requirements and this procedure.

#### Quality Manager

- Maintain the **Record Retention Schedule** and ensure alignment with regulatory requirements (ISO 13485, MDR/IVDR, 21 CFR 820).

- Verify that stored records meet requirements for integrity, traceability, legibility, and retrievability.

- Control the release and protection of electronic records, including access permissions.

- Conduct internal audits to verify compliance with record control requirements.

- Ensure that obsolete records are archived or disposed of according to defined retention periods.

- Ensure compliance with applicable regulatory requirements for record management.

#### Document Control

- Store, organize, and protect records (physical and/or electronic).

- Maintain control of physical and electronic access to stored records.

- Ensure that the location of each record is documented and traceable.

- Apply and monitor retention periods for all records.

- Manage final disposition (long-term archiving or controlled destruction).

- Maintain inventories or listings of stored records (Record Inventory).

#### Regulatory Affairs

- Define or communicate additional regulatory retention requirements.

- Notify QA of regulatory changes affecting the creation, maintenance, or retention of records.

- Ensure that records required for Technical Documentation, Design History File, and regulatory submissions are properly maintained and retrievable.

- Verify that records related to PMS, vigilance, CAPA, UDI, and other regulatory areas meet applicable requirements.

#### Top Management

- Provide sufficient resources for proper record storage, protection, and retrieval.

- Approve the overall policy for record retention and protection.

- Ensure that critical records are maintained in compliance with regulatory standards.

#### Employees / End Users

- Generate and complete records clearly, legibly, indelibly, and using approved forms.

- Submit completed records to the appropriate Document Owner, QA, or Document Control.

- Report any damaged, missing, or incorrect records immediately.
